"Eiffel Tower-Paris"

Standing tall right in the heart of Paris, the Eiffel Tower is a mesmerizing visual gaze. Also, famous as the ‘Iron Lady’, the Eiffel Tower in Paris holds a place in the Seven Wonders of the World. Eiffel Tower tours are available for tourists in both day and night. Enjoying a panoramic view of Paris from the top of the tower is one of the best things to do in Paris. A renowned bridge builder and an expert in metals and architecture, the creative experts of Alexandre-Gustave Eiffel led to the construction of one of its kind architectures, Eiffel Tower. The first digging work of the Eiffel Tower in Paris was started on 28th January 1887.
